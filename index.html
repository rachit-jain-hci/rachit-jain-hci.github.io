About me

I'm a student at IIIT-Delhi graduating in June 2021 looking to join high impact UX and Product teams. I believe in making people's life comfy through research driven design. I work in Human Computer Interaction where I deploy the design process from end-to-end. I picked up design, ethnography and AI with my CS education.

I wear multiple hats- a researcher, designer and leader. My adaptability is my biggest strength- be it visiting the field to interview absolute strangers, designing for VR or convincing supervisors to make changes. I navigate ambiguity and leverage collaboration to understand messy data and innovate ideas.
