About me

I'm a final year student at IIIT-Delhi who wants to make lives comfortable through research driven design. I am working in Human Computer Interaction, designing solutions and alternatives to make people’s experiences with technology seamless.

I picked up projects in human-centered design, ethnography and AI, alongwith my computer science education. I enjoy wearing multiple hats- of a researcher, designer and leader. My adaptability and agency are my biggest strengths- be it visiting the field to interview absolute strangers, designing experiences in VR or advocating for user centered design in programming projects. I like to tackle complex problems, and navigate the ambiguity they present. I enjoy working with stakeholders to synthesize messy data into actionable ideas.

Currently, I am working towards helping people learn a language in mixed reality, researching how the GenZ in Delhi uses and buys sneakers, rekindling my love for dancing, and actively mentoring 60+ undergraduate design students build some amazing interactive systems.
